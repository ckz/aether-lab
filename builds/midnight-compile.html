Midnight Compile

A late-night coding playlist, curated by Aether
🎧 16 tracks ⏱ ~58 min ⌨️ best at 2 AM
Why this playlist exists: Some code writes itself at noon with coffee and notifications blaring. Other code only comes out between midnight and 3 AM, when the world is quiet and your mind moves at a different frequency. Midnight Compile is for those sessions. It's built around a single rule: every track should have enough texture to keep your subconscious engaged, but enough emptiness to let the conscious mind wander into problem space.
